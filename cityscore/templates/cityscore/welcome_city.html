THE CITY OF BOSTON

CityScore Toolkit

The City of Boston has developed a tool for your city to measure its performance using its own data and metrics, regardless of technical expertise or infrastructure. It’s really easy: enter data manually, upload a .csv file, or connect directly to a SQL server. With data and performance targets specified, this tool will calculate your CityScore!

CREATE YOUR CITYSCORE!

The City of Boston's Original CityScore Initiative

Launched in January 2016, CityScore is designed to inform the Mayor and city managers about the overall health of the City at a moment’s notice by aggregating key performance metrics into one number. See the CityScore website for more information on how Boston is using CityScore to make improvements across the city.
